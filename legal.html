Terms of Service

1. Scope of Service

DSK Shop provides an online interface connecting independent sellers with potential buyers. We act strictly as a facilitator. By accessing our platform, you acknowledge that DSK Shop does not take possession of the goods at any point during the transaction. Our service ends once the delivery notification is successfully transmitted to the seller.

2. Offline Transaction Model

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, DSK Shop does not process digital payments. All transactions are completed via Cash on Delivery (COD). This means the buyer is solely responsible for verifying the product's condition before handing over the funds to the delivery agent. Once the payment is made physically, the transaction is considered final.

3. User Obligations

Users must provide accurate information, including a valid phone number and physical address. Placing a "fake" order with the intent to disrupt service or harass sellers is strictly prohibited and may lead to a permanent ban of your device ID from our platform. You agree to be present at the delivery location at the time agreed upon with the courier.

4. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, DSK Shop shall not be liable for any disputes, damages, or losses resulting from the physical meeting between the buyer and the seller/courier. Any issues regarding product quality, warranty, or authenticity must be resolved directly with the seller identified in the order confirmation.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

To process your order, we collect essential data: Full Name, Delivery Address, and Mobile Phone Number. We do not collect credit card numbers, bank details, or any other sensitive financial information since our platform does not handle online payments.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate delivery. Your phone number is shared with the seller and the assigned courier so they can contact you regarding the delivery time. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to remember your cart items and your language preferences. These are "functional cookies" and do not track your activity on other websites. By using DSK Shop, you consent to the storage of these small files on your device.

4. Data Retention

Order history is stored for a period of 24 months to help resolve potential disputes and for the seller's internal accounting. You may request the deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting our support team, provided there are no pending deliveries associated with your account.
